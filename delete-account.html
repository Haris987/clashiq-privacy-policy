Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your ClashIQ account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to harisomerovic987@gmail.com from the email address associated with your ClashIQ account.
  2. Use the subject line: "ClashIQ Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your ClashIQ username in the email body.
Request Account Deletion

What Data Is Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address and login credentials
  • Your username and profile information
  • Your favorite topics and preferences
  • Your QELO ratings and XP progression
  • Your achievements and badges

What Data Is Retained

The following data is retained in anonymized form to preserve the integrity of contests for other players:

  • Historical contest results and leaderboard entries (your username is removed)
  • Contest scores and answer records (disassociated from your identity)

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at harisomerovic987@gmail.com. See our Privacy Policy for more details on how we handle your data.
